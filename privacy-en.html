Effective Date: July 2, 2025

1. Introduction

At LupaQR, your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our application. We comply with applicable data protection laws, including the European Union General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and other relevant regulations in jurisdictions where we operate.

3. Data Collection and Use

LupaQR does not collect, store, or transmit personal data. All scanning happens locally on your device. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, contact information, or device identifiers.
  • We do not collect or track your location.
  • We do not store scanned QR codes or barcodes on our servers.

Camera Access:

LupaQR requires access to your device's camera to scan QR codes and barcodes in real-time. This data is processed only in your device's memory and is never transmitted to us.

Legal Basis for Processing:

We process camera data as necessary to perform the contract with you—to provide the scanning functionality you request.

4. Data Retention

LupaQR does not retain personal data. Any scanned content remains solely on your device and can be removed by clearing your scan history or uninstalling the app.

5. Sharing and Transfers

We do not share, sell, or transfer personal data to third parties. No data leaves your device.

If you choose to open a scanned link, you will be redirected to external websites. Those websites are not controlled by LupaQR and have their own privacy practices.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under applicable privacy laws, including the rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict your personal data.

Please note: LupaQR does not collect, store, or process any personal data on our servers. If you contact us to exercise these rights, we will confirm that no personal data is retained by us.

7. Children's Privacy

LupaQR is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us, and we will promptly delete it.

8. Security Measures

We use appropriate safeguards to protect your information. All scanned data is processed only in your device's memory and is never stored on our servers.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. For material changes, we will notify you through the app or by other appropriate means. Your continued use after such notice means you accept the updated Policy.
